# 🛒 Supermarket Sales Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data from existing **Realmart Supermarket** branches to generate business insights that support the company's expansion into a new city.

Using **Power BI**, the dashboard visualizes key performance indicators (KPIs), customer behavior, product performance, sales trends, and payment preferences. The insights derived from this analysis can help management make data-driven decisions regarding the location, inventory planning, and marketing strategy for the new branch.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Realmart plans to open a new supermarket branch in another city. Before expanding, the company wants to analyze the performance of its existing branches to understand customer preferences, sales patterns, and product performance.

The challenge is to transform raw sales data into an interactive dashboard that provides actionable business insights and recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The main objectives of this project are:

* Analyze supermarket sales performance across existing branches.
* Identify high-performing cities and product categories.
* Understand customer purchasing behavior and payment preferences.
* Evaluate customer satisfaction through ratings.
* Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for business decision-making.
* Support management with data-driven recommendations for opening a new branch.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes the following insights:

* 💰 Total Gross Income

## 📈 Key Insights

* Mumbai generated the highest gross income among all branches.
* Food & Beverages contributed the highest revenue.
* Cash was the most preferred payment method.
* Male customers contributed a larger share of gross income.
* Fashion Accessories received the highest average customer rating.

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations

* Consider opening the next branch in a city with customer demographics similar to the highest-performing branch.
* Increase inventory for top-performing product categories.
* Continue supporting multiple payment options while optimizing for the most popular methods.
* Improve lower-rated product categories through quality improvements and customer feedback.
* Focus marketing campaigns on high-value customer segments.
